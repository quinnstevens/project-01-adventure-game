# The Blackspire Catacombs, a dungeon with 16 rooms
# Author: Quinn Stevens
16
0: Entrance Antechamber: Rough-hewn steps descend into a low stone chamber. A cracked tablet bears a warning in a dead tongue. Cold air whistles from deeper halls.
1: Watcher’s Guard Post: A squat alcove with arrow slits cut into the rock. A broken bronze gong lies on the floor beside a splintered spear rack.
2: Collapsed Hall: The ceiling has caved in here. Rubble forms treacherous footing and the dust tastes like chalk. A faded mural shows a crowned skull.
3: Ossuary Gate: A barred archway fashioned from interlocked femurs. Iron rings hang from chains; someone has pried one bar loose.
4: Dripping Cistern: A circular chamber with a waist-high stone lip. Black water laps quietly; the air smells of rust and wet limestone.
5: Prison Cells: Iron doors line the corridor. Scratches and tally marks cover the walls. A key ring sits just out of reach behind a grate.
6: Abandoned Armory: Rotted leather and tarnished mail spill from busted chests. A rack of knives remains strangely sharp.
7: Chapel of Bones: An altar of skulls faces a cracked obsidian idol. Melted candles have flowed like waxen stalagmites.
8: Flooded Passage: Ankle-deep water ripples with unseen currents. Moss glows faintly along the stone.
9: Mosaic Gallery: The floor depicts heroes battling a serpent. Tiles are missing—forming a serpentine path through the room.
10: Alchemist’s Laboratory: Soot-coated alembics and shattered vials. A chalk circle is scuffed, as if something stepped out.
11: Crypt of the Nameless: Stone sarcophagi line the walls, their faces eroded smooth. A chill steals breath and courage alike.
12: Warden’s Quarters: A narrow bed, a desk with ledgers, and a heavy chest bolted to the floor. The smell of lamp oil lingers.
13: Ritual Chamber: Carved channels converge at a central basin. Dried, flaking residues paint star-like sigils across the flagstones.
14: Treasure Vault: Bronze doors stand ajar. Empty pedestals and dust outlines hint at treasures long removed—save for one sealed coffer.
15: The Peak: A ring of standing stones hums softly. Hair rises on your arms as violet sparks skitter across the air.
